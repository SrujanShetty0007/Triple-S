About Triple S

An open-source platform dedicated to helping engineering students excel in their academic journey

Our Mission

Triple S (Student Study Support) is dedicated to providing a comprehensive, well-organized, and free resource platform for B.E. students. We believe that access to quality study materials should be easy and free for all students, regardless of their background or resources. Our goal is to create a collaborative space where students can find, share, and contribute to a growing collection of notes, model papers, and previous year question papers.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Notes

Access well-organized study notes for each subject, broken down by module to help you understand complex concepts easily.

Model Question Papers

Practice with carefully crafted model question papers designed to help you prepare for your exams effectively.

Previous Year Papers

Get access to a collection of previous year question papers to understand exam patterns and important topics.

Community Driven

Our platform thrives on student contributions, creating a collaborative environment for knowledge sharing.

Device Friendly

Access all materials on any device with our responsive design that works seamlessly on smartphones, tablets, and desktops.

Semester Organized

All materials are neatly organized by semester and subject, making it easy to find what you need when you need it.

Our Story

Triple S started as a simple project by Srujan S Shetty, an engineering student at Moodlakatte Institute of Technology who recognized the challenges faced by fellow students in finding organized study materials. What began as a personal collection of notes and papers has evolved into a comprehensive platform serving students across various engineering disciplines.

The idea was simple: create a clean, minimal, and user-friendly platform where students can quickly access the study materials they need without any distractions or barriers. As more students discovered the platform, it grew through contributions and suggestions from the community.

Today, Triple S continues to expand its collection while maintaining its core values of simplicity, accessibility, and community collaboration.
